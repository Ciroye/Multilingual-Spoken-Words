Multilingual Spoken Words Corpus



Multilingual Spoken Words Corpus is a large and growing audio dataset of spoken words in 50 languages for academic research and commercial applications in keyword spotting and spoken term search, licensed under CC-BY 4.0. Our dataset contains over 350,000 keywords, totaling 17 million 1-second spoken examples(more than 4,000 hours). The dataset has many diverse use cases, ranging from enabling voice-assisted consumer devices to call center detection.
